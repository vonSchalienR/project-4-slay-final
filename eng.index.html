ABOUT US

Established in Turku, in 2023, Froooters has evolved into the premier micromobility platform in Finland, offering a collective electric scooter app. Our vision is to revolutionize urban transportation across the country. At Froooters, we are committed to providing a seamless experience, allowing users to drive scooters from various brands with a single subscription. We believe in empowering individuals with the freedom to choose their preferred electric scooter, providing them with the flexibility to explore cities on their terms. Froooters is dedicated to sustainability, offering an eco-friendly service that surpasses traditional transportation alternatives. Quality, affordability, environmental consciousness, safety, and user-friendliness are fundamental aspects of our business and cultural values. Join Froooters and experience the convenience of diverse electric scooters at your fingertips, all under one subscription.
